Enforcement‑First Field‑Service OS

An operations platform for an electrical, security, AV & telecom group — dispatch, workforce, assets, procurement and dual-sided billing behind one decision engine.

RoleSole Architect / Full-Stack
TimelineMay 2026 — Present
ClientAustralian multi-entity trade group
Shape16-workspace Turborepo · 3 apps
The Brief

Stop margin leakage before it ships.

Unbilled supplier costs and scheduled-vs-actual billing gaps were leaking an estimated $5k a week. ETG puts one pure, unit-testable decision gate between the API and every workflow — every dispatch, closeout and invoice transition is evaluated, and every block or manager override lands in an audit trail.

Dispatch board — 8-check enforcement gate + manager overrides
Workforce — timesheets, closeouts, licences, AU award engine
Supplier billing + scored two-hop bank reconciliation, with golden-rule gates so a job can't reach ready-to-invoice until its costs are accounted for
Asset / inventory register, quoting, service tickets — one 106+-model schema
